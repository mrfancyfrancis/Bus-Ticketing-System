About Us


Bus.Logo was established on the year 2020 when the COVID-19 pandemic was at peak. As the "New Normal" comes in town, public transporation is slowly coming back. Health of is great importance and social distancing is the new trend in "New Normal". People now favors online transactions as the new means to support their purchases and other services. The company strives to keep the bus transportation services alive and easily accesible to the people amidst the pandemic.
